## 中文說明

### 什麼是每日浪漫驚喜？

「每日浪漫驚喜」是專為情侶與伴侶設計的互動工具，每天隨機提供 **12 則驚喜點子**，從約會計畫、溫柔的話、小禮物驚喜到肢體關心，幫助你輕鬆維持關係中的儀式感與浪漫氣氛。

無論你是長期伴侶想重燃熱情、新戀人想製造甜蜜回憶，還是想在日常中加一點「用心」，這款工具都適合你。

---

### 核心功能

#### 🌸 探索驚喜（每日 12 則）

- 每天隨機洗牌 12 則驚喜卡片，靈感不重複
- **左滑** 看上一則，**右滑** 看下一則，循環瀏覽
- 每張卡片可個別操作：
  - **收藏 ♡** — 喜歡的點子先存起來
  - **標記執行** — 記錄你真的做了！自動記錄日期
  - **取消執行** — 反悔也沒關係
- 點擊「今日驚喜」可手動刷新重排（收藏與記錄不受影響）
- 即時統計：**已收藏 · 已執行 · 今日驚喜數**

#### 💌 我的收藏（永久保存）

- 集中管理所有收藏與執行記錄
- 三欄篩選統計一目了然：
  - **已收藏** — 全部收藏（含未執行 + 已執行）
  - **未執行** — 還沒行動的點子清單
  - **已執行** — 已完成，永久保存並顯示執行日期
- 同樣支援左右滑動卡片瀏覽
- 已執行項目顯示完成日期與刪除線標示

### 使用規則

- 已執行的點子**永久保存**，不會消失，留作美好記憶

- 刷新今日驚喜只重排順序，所有收藏與執行記錄完整保留

### 常見問題

**Q：每天的驚喜都一樣嗎？**  
A：不會。每次載入或刷新都會重新隨機洗牌，12 則的順序每次都不同。

**Q：關掉頁面後收藏會消失嗎？**  
A：目前為瀏覽器記憶體儲存，關閉分頁後資料會清空。建議使用前先截圖記錄喜歡的點子。

**Q：可以自己新增驚喜內容嗎？**  
A：目前版本為固定 12 則，未來版本預計支援自訂內容。

## English Description

### What is Daily Romantic Surprise?

*Daily Romantic Surprise* is an interactive tool built for couples and partners. Every day it serves up **12 randomly shuffled surprise ideas** — spanning date plans, heartfelt words, little gifts, and caring gestures — to help you effortlessly maintain romance and intentionality in your relationship.

Whether you're a long-term couple looking to reignite the spark, new partners building sweet memories, or simply someone who wants to show up more thoughtfully for the person they love — this is for you.

---

### Core Features

#### 🌸 Explore Surprises (12 per day)

- 12 surprise cards reshuffled randomly every day — fresh ideas, no repetition
- **Swipe left** for the previous card, **swipe right** for the next — loops infinitely
- Each card has independent controls:
  - **Save ♡** — bookmark ideas you love
  - **Mark as Done** — log that you actually did it, with today's date auto-recorded
  - **Undo Done** — change your mind anytime
- Tap "Today's Surprises" to manually reshuffle (saves and records stay intact)
- Live stats: **Saved · Done · Today's Total**

#### 💌 My Collection (Permanent Records)

- Manage all saved and completed surprises in one place
- Three filterable stat blocks with instant counts:
  - **Saved** — everything saved (pending + done)
  - **Pending** — ideas saved but not yet acted on
  - **Done** — completed, permanently stored with execution date
- Swipe left/right to browse saved cards
- Completed cards show a strikethrough title and the date it was done

---

### Four Surprise Categories

| Category | Description | Best For |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| 💜 **Date Idea** | Outings or at-home date plans | Weekends, anniversaries, memory-making |
| 💚 **A Word for Today** | One gentle, heartfelt thing to say | Any moment — just say it |
| 🩷 **Little Surprise** | A small unexpected gesture | Brightening an ordinary day |
| 🟡 **Small Action** | Free, physical acts of care | Daily life, bedtime, after work |

---

### Rules

- Completed items are **permanently saved** — they stay forever as beautiful memories
- A completed item **cannot be un-saved** until you first undo its done status
- Refreshing today's surprises only reshuffles the order — all saves and records are untouched

### FAQ

**Q: Are the surprises different every day?**  
A: Yes. Every load or manual refresh reshuffles the 12 cards into a new random order.

**Q: Will my saves disappear if I close the page?**  
A: Currently the app stores data in browser memory, so closing the tab clears the session. We recommend screenshotting ideas you love before closing.

**Q: Can I add my own surprise ideas?**  
A: Not in the current version. Custom content support is planned for a future release.
