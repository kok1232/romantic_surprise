# 💕 每日浪漫驚喜 Daily Romantic Surprise

> 為你們的每一天加點甜 · A little sweetness for every day together

---

## 中文說明

### 簡介

「每日浪漫驚喜」是一款為伴侶設計的互動小工具，每天提供 12 則隨機驚喜點子，涵蓋約會計劃、溫柔的話、小驚喜與肢體關心四大類別，讓日常生活多一點儀式感與浪漫氣氛。

### 功能說明

#### 探索驚喜

- 每日隨機排列 12 則驚喜卡片
- 左滑查看上一則，右滑查看下一則，循環無限
- 每張卡片可單獨操作：
  - **收藏 ♡** — 將驚喜加入收藏清單
  - **標記執行** — 記錄已實際執行，自動鎖定收藏並記錄日期
  - **取消執行** — 撤銷執行記錄
- 點擊「今日驚喜」方塊可手動刷新，重新洗牌 12 則內容（收藏與執行記錄不受影響）
- 上方三格統計：**已收藏 / 已執行 / 今日驚喜**

#### 我的收藏

- 顯示所有已收藏或已執行的驚喜
- 上方三格篩選統計：
  - **已收藏** — 全部收藏數量（含未執行與已執行）
  - **未執行** — 收藏但尚未行動的數量
  - **已執行** — 已完成並永久保存的數量
- 點擊任一方塊即可切換篩選，並以卡片形式左右滑動瀏覽
- 已執行項目顯示執行日期，標題加上刪除線

### 驚喜類別

| 類別 | 說明 |
|------|------|
| 💜 約會點子 | 適合兩人共度的外出或居家約會 |
| 💚 今日一句話 | 溫柔說出口的一句心裡話 |
| 🩷 小驚喜 | 讓對方意外感動的小舉動 |
| 🟡 小動作 | 不需要成本的肢體關心 |

### 規則說明

- 已執行的項目**永久保存**，不會消失
- 已執行的項目**無法取消收藏**，只能先「取消執行」後才可解除
- 刷新今日驚喜只重排卡片順序，不影響任何收藏或執行記錄

## English Description

### Overview

*Daily Romantic Surprise* is an interactive widget designed for couples. It presents 12 randomly shuffled surprise ideas each day across four categories — date ideas, kind words, little surprises, and affectionate gestures — to bring more romance and intentionality to everyday life.

### Features

#### Explore Surprises

- 12 surprise cards are randomly shuffled each day
- Swipe **left** for the previous card, swipe **right** for the next — loops infinitely
- Each card supports independent actions:
  - **Save ♡** — add the surprise to your saved list
  - **Mark as Done** — log that you actually did it; locks the save and records today's date
  - **Undo Done** — remove the done status
- Tap the **"Today's Surprises"** stat block to manually refresh and reshuffle the 12 cards (saves and done records are unaffected)
- Three stat blocks at the top: **Saved / Done / Today's Surprises**

#### My Collection

- Displays all saved or completed surprises
- Three filterable stat blocks at the top:
  - **Saved** — total saved count (pending + done)
  - **Pending** — saved but not yet acted on
  - **Done** — completed items, permanently preserved with date
- Tap any block to switch the filter; browse cards by swiping left or right
- Completed cards show the execution date and a strikethrough title

### Surprise Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| 💜 Date Idea | Fun outings or cosy at-home date plans |
| 💚 A Word for Today | One gentle, heartfelt thing to say out loud |
| 🩷 Little Surprise | A small unexpected gesture to touch their heart |
| 🟡 Small Action | A no-cost physical act of care and closeness |

### Rules

- Completed items are **permanently saved** and cannot be deleted
- A completed item **cannot be un-saved** unless you first undo its done status
- Refreshing today's surprises only reshuffles card order — all saves and done records remain intact
